When should I call my doctor?

You should also contact your doctor if:
  1. Your waters break or if you suspect you're leaking amniotic fluid.
  2. Your baby is moving les than usual.
  3. You have any vaginal bleeding (unless it's just a small amount of blood-tinged mucus)
  4. You have a fever, severe headaches, changes in your vision, or abdominal pain.
