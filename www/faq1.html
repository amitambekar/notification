Time it takes to get pregnant?

Normally fertile couples have a 20 to 30 percent chance of getting pregnant each cycle. About 84% of couples in the general population will conceive within one year if they have unprotected, regular sex, and half of the remainder will conceive naturally within the following year, if they keep trying. This means that while some couples will get pregnant in the first month of trying, for other couples it may take a couple of years before they conceive. Even then. some healthy couples can take even longer to get pregnant.
